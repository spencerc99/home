> _I'm looking for an apprentice / studio assistant to help me create tangible, playful, and intimate technology-infused art that can be experienced by people around the world. Will you join me?_

## Who am I & what do I do?

I like to say I make software you can touch. In other words, I care about creating software, spaces, and sculptures that push the limits of tangibility, liveness, and care through technology, the internet, and our devices.

here's my official bio to give you an idea of how I project "officially:"

Spencer Chang is an artist, engineer, and toy maker interested in the play, creation, and care that emerge from our relationships with and through technological systems. Working across internet spaces, interactive sculpture, and creative infrastructure, they engage with everyday practices and material to reconstruct technology as a medium for communal flourishing. These works leverage whimsical intimacy and critical joy to interrogate our systems, invite collective imaginations, and provide the means to reinvent them.

- Chang's work has been showcased by the Gray Area & the de Young Museum (San Francisco), Tokyo Geidai (Tokyo), Museum of the Moving Image (New York), Ars Electronica (Linz), and the National Communication Museum (Melbourne).
- Created commissions for Hyundai Artlab, Alserkal Avenue, the Internet Archive, and Wikipedia
- Grants & support from the San Francisco Arts Commission, Rhizome, the Ethereum Foundation, New Aesthetics, and NEW INC.
- Projects featured in MIT Tech Review, The Cut, It's Nice That, and Frieze

## The Role!

This is a new role and you will have a lot of responsibility and latitude in shaping it.

- **this would be a good fit if you:**
  - want to make software, objects, and installations that represent human-centered, playful, intimate technology
  - are creative and technical. you've made things as creative outlets for yourself and made or designed products, tools, or other things that are used regularly by other people.
  - feel comfortable working mostly autonomously and driving your own projects forward
  - like to learn by doing and want to be hands-on on art installations and software projects that are experienced by people around the world and get mentorship from a working artist!
  - want to do something independently & learn skills across the spectrum of software making, physical making, and connecting to people! I'm modeling this after the kind of thing I wished I could have had when I started working independently 4 years ago.
- **commitments:**
  - ~20 hours/wk for 8-10 weeks
  - mostly in-person in my studio in the inner sunset in SF, but some can be remote / hybrid for the right person
- **the kinds of things you might be responsible for:**
  - driving new features / websites built on top of [playhtml](/creation/playhtml) / [we were online](/creation/we-were-online) that experiment with new ways for people to play in and make digital space feel like theirs
  - helping with fabrication and design of in-person exhibits and installations for projects like [Computing Shrines](https://spencer.place/creation/computing-shrines)
  - trying new, weird social media videos and experimenting with new participatory art forms on top of it (see [sharing stories](/creation/sharing-stories) and my [instagram](https://www.instagram.com/spence.r.chang/))
- **Paid!** ($20-30/hr based on experience)
